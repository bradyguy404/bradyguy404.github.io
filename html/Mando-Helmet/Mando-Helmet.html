Mandalorian Helmet

"I can bring you in warm or I can bring you in cold." - Dinn Djarin

Planning
Perhaps one of the most iconic images from Star Wars are the helmets from various characters. One of these many iconic helmets is that of the Mandalorian bounty hunter turned reluctant father figure Dinn Djarin. The helmet is a shiny chrome color complete with a built in flashlight and a tinted visor. This particular project is one that I selected as the geometry of the helmet is something that I have not really had to model in the past and thus thought it would be good practice for these more complex geometries. I also figured the helmet should have a flashlight that can be manually turned on just like it does in the show The Mandalorian. I would also try and match the color and have a transparent tinted visor that the wearer can see through but someone looking at the helmet wouldn't be able to see through.
