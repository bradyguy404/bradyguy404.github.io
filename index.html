Below is a list of all my work, both in progress and completed. Each entry contains a picture and a small description. If you would like to know more about a piece or see additional photos click on the section containing that piece.




The Ace of Spades
A 3D printed replica of the Ace of Spades hand cannon from the game Destiny 2. This weapon replica does not fire a projectile though the trigger does rotate the cylinder exactly 60º as if chambering another one of the 6 shots within the cylinder. When the trigger is fully pulled back the hammer will release as if firing a round. The wielder may also fire the weapon by cocking the hammer which rotates the cylinder and sets the hammer to be released upon a successful full trigger pull. Additionally, the weapon's cylinder will glow a faint bluish light when a certain ammo cell is inserted within the cylinder.


The Master Sword
A 3D printed replica of the Master Sword from Legend of the Zelda. This particular replica of the Master Sword is modeled after its appearance within the game Breath of the Wild. The replica has been slightly modifed from its original appearance within the game. The blade has been thinned down and the grip us more cylindrical to ensure the blade is in-line with where the wielder desires. The sword is technically classified as a longsword with a blade length of 38 inches and the wings of the guard spanning 12 inches.


Mini Metal Foundry
A small homemade metal foundry with a kale wool insulation and fire brick interior structure. The foundry is propane powered and is mainly used for melting and casting with aluminum.


Mandalorian Helmet
A 3D printed replica of the helmet of Dinn Djarin from the Disney+ show The Mandalorian. This particular helmet is scaled to fit my head.


Chef's Knife
A hand forged chef's knife made of 5160 steel with a stone wash finish. The blade measures in at a length of 8 1⁄4 inches with a blade thickness of 1⁄8 in.


Future Projects



Gobblegum Machine (Status: Planned)
This future project will be a to scale version of the Gobblegum gumball machine from the game Call of Duty: Black Ops III. The machine will be fully functioning and accept nickels in order to dispense gobblegums. The machine has lights that will blink while the machine is idle and when dispensing a gumball the glass ball will light up blue and play a sound similar to how it does within the game.


R2-D2 Astromech Droid (Status: Planned)
This future project will be a to scale version of the R2-D2 from the Star Wars franchise. The intial model will have a retractable leg and will be able to remotely controlled to move around. The droid will also be able to rotate its head and make sounds as it does within the movies. The droid will be complete with working lights and flaps that can be manually opened.
