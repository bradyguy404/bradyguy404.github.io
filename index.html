Below is a list of all my work, both in progress and completed. Each entry contains a picture and a small description. If you would like to know more about a piece click on the overview section of that piece.
The Ace of Spades
A 3D printed replica of the Ace of Spades hand cannon from the game Destiny 2. This replica is complete with a double action hammer-trigger mechanism with a rotatating cylinder.


The Master Sword
A 3D printed replica of the Master Sword from Legend of the Zelda. This particular replica is modeled after its appearance within the game Breath of the Wild.


Mini Metal Foundry
A small homemade metal foundry with a kale wool insulation and fire brick interior structure. The foundry is propane powered and is mainly used for melting and casting with aluminum.


Mandalorian Helmet
A 3D printed replica of the helmet of Dinn Djarin from the Disney+ show The Mandalorian. This particular helmet is scaled to fit my head.
